# **Privacy Policy**

## 1. **Introduction**

This Privacy Policy outlines how Discord Wordle Stats collects, uses, stores, and protects data related to its operation as a Discord bot. By using Discord Wordle Stats, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. **Data Collection**

At the time of writing, Discord Wordle Stats collects the following data from Discord guilds:

- **Guild ID:** Used to uniquely identify a guild.
- **Channel IDs:** Identifies channels where stats have been gathered.
- **User IDs:** Stores user IDs that have played the Wordle game.
- **User nicknames:** Stores guild nicknames of users that played the Wordle game.
- **Mesage content:** Only stores message content authored by the [official Wordle application](https://discord.com/users/1211781489931452447).

All collected data is related to the guilds and channels, using information provided by Discord's API, which is publicly visible on the platform.

## 3. **Data Usage**

The data collected by Discord Wordle Stats is used to calculate Wordle score averages and correlate users to nicknames. This data is essential for the bot to function as intended, offering accurate and quick statistics summaries.

## 4. **Data Storage**

- The collected data is stored in a PouchDB database hosted on a server maintained by the developer.
- The data is securely stored with password protection. However, please note that the data itself is not encrypted.

## 5. **Data Sharing**

Discord Wordle Stats does not share the collected data with any third parties. The data is used solely within the bot to provide the necessary features and services to the guilds.

## 6. **Data Retention**

The data collected by Discord Wordle Stats is retained indefinitely unless a guild owner or authorized representative requests its deletion. Requests for data removal can be made by contacting the developer directly (see Section 9 for contact information).

## 7. **Security Measures**

- The server and database hosting the collected data are password protected to prevent unauthorized access.
- While measures are in place to protect the data, it is important to note that the data itself is not encrypted.

## 8. **User Rights**

Guild owners or their authorized representatives can request access to, modification, or deletion of their guild's data. To make such a request, please contact the developer using the contact information provided below.

## 10. **Changes to the Privacy Policy**

Changes to this Privacy Policy may be made at any time. However, users will not be notified of changes to the policy. It is recommended that users review the Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
